Biography

Jae is a Korean - American musician-composer, rapper, singer and songwriter. He is the lead vocalist, electric guitarist as well as the face of the South Korean rock band called Day6. Born in Argentina, he spent most of his growing up years in California, US. He graduated from Cerritos High School and later studied political science at California State University, Long Beach. Throughout high school and university, he ran a YouTube channel, "YellowPostItMan", where he uploaded vocal-and-guitar song covers as a hobby. While studying at the ‘California State University,’ he cleared the auditions of the South Korean reality competition, ‘K-pop Star,’ and moved to South Korea. He made to the list of final six contestants and was signed on by the ‘JYP Entertainment,’ for their first rock band, ‘Day6.’ Jae Park debuted in September 2015 with the band’s first EP, ‘The Day’.

In September 2017, Jae started a Youtube channel called JaeSix (later renamed eaJ).He began a personal music project on the channel in 2020, releasing self-written solo tracks under the name eaJ. On February 5, 2020, Jae started his first podcast "How Did I Get Here?" (HDIGH) with Dive Studios. Jae was published some songs by himself and did the collaboration with 88rising to make some videos and put it on his channel
